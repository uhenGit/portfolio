Tech stack:
  • Vue 3 (composition api)
  • Vuetify
  • Vuelidate
  • Vue router

Introducing a sleek and secure login and paint application built with Vue 3, Vuetify, Vuelidate, and Vue Router, designed to provide users with a seamless and intuitive experience from authentication to creative expression. Step into a world of simplicity and functionality as you explore the robust features of this innovative web application.

Begin your journey with a login page that offers robust validation, ensuring only valid emails and six-digit passwords are accepted, providing users with peace of mind and security. Once authenticated, users are ushered into a creative realm where they can unleash their imagination with a unique paint feature.

With Vue Router facilitating smooth navigation, users seamlessly transition between login and paint interfaces, enjoying a cohesive and engaging user experience. Harnessing the power of Vuetify's sleek design elements, the paint interface offers a visually appealing canvas where users can input their desired dimensions and unleash their creativity with precision.
